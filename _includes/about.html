About


Ed Hamel has over 40 years of experience in the home construction industry. Prior to founding Hamel Inspections, he was the owner of Edward P. Hamel General Contractors, Inc. where he led the construction of homes, condominium projects, and other construction jobs. Ed's experience will give you the confidence to make informed decisions in purchasing your new home.

Hamel Inspections is focused on serving Northampton, Amherst, Southampton, Easthampton, Westhampton, Hatfield, and Holyoke.
