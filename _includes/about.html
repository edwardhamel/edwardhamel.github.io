About


Ed Hamel has over 40 years of experience in the home construction industry. Prior to founding Hamel Inspections, he was the owner of Edward P. Hamel General Contractors, Inc. where he led the construction of homes, condominium projects, and other construction jobs. Ed's experience will give you the confidence to make informed decisions in purchasing your new home or condo.

Based in Southampton, we have inspected homes across Western Massachusetts, including those in Northampton, Amherst, Southampton, Easthampton, Westhampton, Hatfield, Holyoke, Greenfield, and the Hilltowns.
